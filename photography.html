Photography Gallery

Explore the first group of my photography collection, where I capture fleeting moments with a camera that holds sentimental value. Each photograph is a testament to my journey in mastering the art of visual storytelling, starting with the gift of a camera from my uncle. While I'm still honing my skills, I find joy in preserving moments and enhancing them through editing. Through these photographs, I strive to convey emotions and narratives, hoping to improve and create lasting memories through my lens

Step into the second group of my photography collection, where I explore the art of capturing cosplay characters brought to life by talented friends. Over the past three years, cosplay has become not just a hobby but a passion that drives my creativity behind the lens. Collaborating with friends who share my love for cosplay, I've ventured into cosplay photography to capture the essence and craftsmanship of characters. Through these photographs, I aim to showcase the dedication and artistry of cosplayers while refining my skills in composition and storytelling. Join me on this journey through the colorful and imaginative world of cosplay, where every click of the shutter captures a character's story and the joy of creative collaboration.
